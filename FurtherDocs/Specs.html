Existing Projects

The room has a lot to do and a lot of PCs with varying specs. Here are the current specs for all the builds and their uses.

All applicable documentation will be linked here and is primarily developed by whoever is in charge of the project itself

    General Work Pcs

    These are very mid tier budget systems, capable of light gaming and workloads. Use these primarily for testout/cisco work and feel free to make changes to them with permission from Mr. Eno.

  • AMD Ryzen 5 1600x

    • AIO Coolers are old and should be replaced

  • 8GB or 16GB of DDR4 2133MHz single/dual channel RAM

    • Most of them are running single channel

  • Nvidia Low-profile GTX-1050 ti
  • 1TB 2.5" HHD

    • Needs to be upgraded to SSD

  • MSI B350 PC Mate
  • Thermaltake smart 80+ bronze 650w PSU

    The Show PC

    The top of the line PC used to look good and perform better.

  • AMD Ryzen 7 5700x
  • 16 GB dual channel DDR4 2666MHz
  • Nvidia GTX 1060 6GB
  • WD RED 2x 3.5" 2TB HDD running in Raid 0
  • 2.5" 128GB SATA SSD, Boot drive
  • M.2 SATA 128GB SSD

    • MoBo doesn't support this M.2 but it's still installed

  • EVGA 650GA 650w 80+ gold

    The Sleeper PC
